How Food Is Provided To Us

How Do We Get The Food We Eat Every Day?

Well there are several ways we aquire the food we need for sustinence nowadays. Whilst most of the food we eat is processed, some food still requires cooking and treatment in order to become editable. Some mushrooms need to be cooked or else they're poisonous, venemous creatures such as lionfish need to be treated carefully in order to avoid digesting or being impaled with any venom.

Is The Culinary Profession An Easy Job?

Many chefs and culinary enthusiasts out there make cooking look like an easy job, but handling dozens of people's plates an hour, and having the skills to cook takes a lot of effort. That being said some cooking jobs are easier than others, but it's no profession to take lightly.
