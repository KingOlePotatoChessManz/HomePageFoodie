How Culture Can Affect The Food We Eat

Traditional Values And Food

Tradition can have a very serious impact on food. There's many people in the world who limit themselves due to traditions.

Food is no exception to that, as Catholics and Monks are two different types of people known to have limited their food intake.

In thw instance of Catholics they limit themselves to poor food, mainly the nuns. Whilst monks don't eat anything during a certain time of day.

Get Togethers And Food

There are many holidays and get togethers that are known to americans, Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Years, Weddings, Funerals, and much much more.

A very important part of the these get togethers is the food involved in them and how it brings people together, definetly leading to worthwhile times.
