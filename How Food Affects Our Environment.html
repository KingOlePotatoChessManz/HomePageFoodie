How Food Affects Our Environment

What Impact Can Food Have On Our Surroundings?

Beleive it or not the main cause of deforestation is agricultural expansion. While it may be justified to grow more crops to feed the hungry, at the same time it's cooking our atmosphere. This is a very real issue that needs a real solution, as deforestation is not a wise choice in the long run.

What Does Food Draw In?

Believe it or not a lot of people will travel to places and go out of their way to try a new dish. Food can definetly be a driving factor towards tourism and a communities level of thriving conditions.
