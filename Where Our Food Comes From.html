Where Our Food Comes From

What Places Supply Us With Food?

There are many places in the world that supply us with food, from the home kitchen, to restaurants, grocery stores, and so fourth. But where does our food origionate from? Well our food just so happens to mainly be sourced off of farms managed by farmhands. Now this might sound like a narrow topic, but actually there's many different types of farms that farmers tend to manage. There's crop farms, animal farms, fish farms, hunting ranges, tree fields, algae farms, and many other types of farms out there.

How Does Our Food Get Supplied To Us?

Often times the places where our food comes from are not within the same state that us Americans live in. So how does our food travel? Our food gets to us through the transportation services such as semis, vans, boats, trains, planes, drones, horseback and other modes of travel.
