The Impact of Food On The Economy

What Influences Did Food Have In Regards To Economics?

Food used to have a massive influence over the economy. Back in the Medieval Era's food was above gold in trading value. Native Americans considered food to be the only valuable trading commodity for a certain amount of time. And there is many famous literacy works about how gold has no value to a starving man in reference to how valued food can be.

What Influence Does Food Have In Regards To The Current Economy?

In regards to the current economy the value of food has significantly reduced since the Industrial Age came about. But that does not mean that food is not still a valuable commodity in some way, as it is quite essential for human survival. In fact there is still many places in the world such as Afric, India, and China where starvation is a real issue for the people.
