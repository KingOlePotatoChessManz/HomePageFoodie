How Our Food Is Treated

What's The Procedure For Treating Food?

Genrally when preparing food there isn't any one solution to properly make sure a meal comes out without violating a regulation of some kind. But some common things to note for when preparing food is to always wash hands before and after cooking or touching something inately poisonous. It's important to note that there's many prior events and sceintific discoveries that led up to the knowledge of treating food we have now.

What Happens When Food Isn't Treated?

Germs are on everything, those are the first issue with not treating food properly by washing hands as they can easily spread sickness. Then there's determining how to make food editable, throughout history people have been known to eat food raw and experience harshness. But it's by that harshness and the knowledge they and others gained from experiencing that harshness that we can better treat food in the modern day.
