The Effect of Food On Health

Believe it or not eating healthy is not neccessarily being healthy, but in the general sceme of things it helps a lot.

Many medical conditions probability of happing is drastically affected by the food someone eats, ate, and or isn't eating.

Food Ingredients

Foods that are considered rather high in healthy vitamins include fruits, vegetables, meats, dairy, nuts, and seeds.

Whilst foods that are considered rather poor in vitameins often include high amounts of fats, salts, and sugars. The healthy foods are the ones that typically decrease the chances of most medical diagnosisis, with the oppoiste for the unhealthy foods.
